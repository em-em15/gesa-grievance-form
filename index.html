System Purpose

The sole purpose of the GESA Grievance Form is to ensure that all student concerns, complaints, and grievances are properly documented, acknowledged, and given due attention. Every submission is treated with importance and respect, ensuring that no concern is ignored or left unheard. Through this system, all grievances are reviewed and acted upon accordingly to promote accountability, transparency, and continuous improvement within the Geodetic Engineering community.
