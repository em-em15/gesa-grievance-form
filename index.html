GESA Geodetic Engineering Grievance System

Purpose of the System

The sole purpose of the GESA Grievance Form is to ensure that all student concerns, complaints, and grievances are properly documented, acknowledged, and given due attention. Through this system, every submission is treated with importance and respect, ensuring that no concern is ignored or left unheard. It serves as a structured channel between students and the organization, enabling transparency, accountability, and continuous improvement within the Geodetic Engineering community. Ultimately, the information gathered through this platform will guide responsible actions, appropriate interventions, and informed decision-making aimed at addressing student needs effectively.
